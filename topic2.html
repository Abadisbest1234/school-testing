Kasane Teto

The Cyan Vocaloid

About Kasane Teto

Kasane Teto is a Vocaloid character known for her distinctive cyan and pink appearance and cheerful personality. Originally created as a joke character, she has become beloved by the Vocaloid community for her unique voice and charming character design. Her voice provider, Saki Fujita, gives her a bright and energetic sound that perfectly matches her vibrant aesthetic.

Character Traits

  • Cheerful and energetic personality
  • Known for her distinctive high-pitched voice
  • Signature cyan and pink color scheme
  • Often depicted with a playful and mischievous attitude
  • Popular in fan music and animations
  • Strong presence in the Vocaloid community
  • Versatile performer across multiple music genres

Cultural Impact
